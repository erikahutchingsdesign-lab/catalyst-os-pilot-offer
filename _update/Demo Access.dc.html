Summary

Three ways this platform creates value

01
Faster, with fewer resources
  • Cuts marketing-deliverable production time by 85–90%
  • Repetitive production work is automated, so one person does what used to take a team — at the same quality
90% production time reduction
70% smaller team that is AI-enabled
02
A “powered by” platform agencies can choose to resell
  • Agencies white-label it to their clients as a subscription
  • Agency designs the deliverable; the client self-updates the deliverables themselves in your proprietary system — e.g. refreshing their own quarterly fact sheet
  • Deliverables built on the agency's design systems, with data integration — no separate tool and no third-party vendor
  • The agency earns recurring revenue
03
Deliverables become an owned, recurring asset
  • Each deliverable stops being a one-off
  • Deliverables are still your designs, your style, your voice — or opt for the system to help you
  • Powered by live, connected data, each asset is digital and created as code — a living, owned asset your team or your client returns to and refreshes
  • Latest numbers pull in automatically rather than requiring manual updates
  • This turns one-time production into an ongoing, recurring relationship
  • If a client leaves the subscription, they still walk away with a hard asset — a PDF, Canva, PPTX, InDesign file, or other output type
